Empanadas

Argentine empanadas are a traditional dish, a dough wrapper (usually made of flour) filled with various ingredients, such as meat, chicken, vegetables, or cheese, which is then baked or fried. They are a symbol of Argentine cuisine and are enjoyed throughout the country, with regional variations in fillings and preparation. - USD 2

Asado

The Argentine asado is a traditional barbecue, a culinary and social ritual deeply rooted in Argentine culture, which consists of grilling meat, primarily beef, over hot coals. It's an emblematic dish of Argentine gastronomy that goes beyond food, representing a gathering of family or friends, where grilled meat is enjoyed alongside drinks and company. - USD 20
